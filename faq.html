Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about delivery, returns, sizes, and orders.

1) How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery usually takes 2–5 working days within the UK. Delivery times may vary during busy periods.

2) How much is delivery?

Delivery costs are shown at checkout. Special offers may include free delivery on selected items.

3) Can I return an item?

Yes. Items can be returned within 14 days if they are unused and in original condition. Please keep your proof of purchase.

4) How do I choose the correct size?

Use our Size Guide page to compare UK and EU shoe sizes and check clothing measurements before ordering.
