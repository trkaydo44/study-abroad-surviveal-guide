About LUKAS

LUKAS is a modern fashion website created to showcase stylish shoes and clothing with a clean and professional online design. The goal of this project is to provide a simple shopping experience with clear navigation, attractive visuals, and responsive layout.

This website was developed as part of a Website Design & Creation project. It demonstrates planning, layout design, image management, navigation structure, and interactive features such as product pages and a contact form.

The black and gold colour theme was selected to create a premium fashion appearance, while consistent headers and footers ensure usability across all pages.

LUKAS focuses on modern style, simplicity, and accessibility for users browsing fashion products online.
