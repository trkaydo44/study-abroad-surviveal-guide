Canada Guide

Practical advice for settling in Canada.

Housing
  • Ask what bills are included (heating is important in winter).
  • Check travel time to campus before you sign.
Winter costs
  • Buy a good coat and boots (safe for ice).
  • Plan extra budget for winter transport and heating.
Transport
  • Ask your university about student transit passes.
  • Use maps to learn routes before your first day.
Food & culture
  • Join student groups to find cultural food options.
  • Try local community markets for cheaper groceries.
