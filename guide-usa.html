United States Guide

Quick tips for first weeks in the USA.

SIM & internet
  • Check student plans and prepaid options.
  • Campus Wi-Fi is good but you still need a SIM for travel.
Campus life
  • Join 1–2 clubs (it helps with homesickness).
  • Use international office support — they’re helpful.
Budgeting
  • Cook more often; eating out adds up fast.
  • Ask about student discounts in stores.
Food (halal / cultural)
  • Check local student groups and community centers.
  • Search for “halal market” + your city.
