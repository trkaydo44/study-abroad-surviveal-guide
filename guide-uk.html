United Kingdom Guide

Simple starter checklist for international students.

Bank account
  • Bring passport + BRP (if you have it) + proof of address.
  • Ask your university for a student letter if needed.
Transport
  • Get a railcard (16–25 or student) for train discounts.
  • In London: apply for Student Oyster to save money.
Health (GP)
  • Register with a local GP as soon as possible.
  • Keep your NHS number safe once you get it.
Budget & groceries
  • Aldi/Lidl are usually cheaper.
  • Use student discounts (UNiDAYS / Student Beans).
